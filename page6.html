Dear Colleagues,
It is my big pleasure and honor to invite You to participate in the "INNN–JHH GLOBAL NEUROLOGICAL DISEASES PARTNERSHIP SYMPOSIUM 2018", which will take place on October 1st-3rd, 2018 in Mexico City, Mexico. The conference is organized by the National Institutes of Health Mexico and Instituto Nacional de Neurología y Neurocirugía “Manuel Velasco Suárez. The conference will take place at the conference hall.
The main advantage of the conference is its the collaborative scientific research with John Hopkins in accordance with the latest advances in Neuro-Pathology: WHO classification review board , Neuro-Critical Care: Current perspectives and new approaches for Neurological end of life decisions Neurosurgery: Neuro-Oncology: Neuro-Immunology , with parallel scientific sessions, enough planned time for discussion, specialized topics focused on clinical research on brain tumor diseases and cancer therapy. During the Meeting the lectures will be presented by internationally recognized experts and will cover recent advances in teh field. The conference provides unique opportunity for students and young investigators to present posters and be selected for short talks. Collegial environment will foster interdisciplinary cross-talks and spark new scientific collaborations.
